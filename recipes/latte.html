Dirty Chai Latte

Delicately sweetened with honey, this dirty chai latte with milk foam is the perfect way to start your mornings

Warm and comforting, this Dirty Chai Latte is the perfect winter drink to get you through rushed mornings. Packed with black tea and an espresso shot, every sip is a caffeine bomb that will keep you wide awake through even the most tedious meetings.

With creamy milk foam and ground cinnamon, this drink sounds harder to make than it is. You only need 20 minutes to make it from start to finish— most of which is spent steeping the tea. Even if you don’t have that time, you’ll be glad to know it can be easily made in advance.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Black tea bags
  • Milk
  • Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Boil the water and add the two tea bags. Cover the saucepan and let the tea steep for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the milk to a small pot over medium heat until it steams. Remove it from the heat and use a frother to foam it.
  3. Remove the tea bags and divide the tea between 2 coffee mugs.
  4. Add in the milk and coffee. Stir to combine. Sweeten it with sugar to taste. Sprinkle it with cinnamon and serve warm.
